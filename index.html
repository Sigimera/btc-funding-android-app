CrowdFunding On-The-Go.
No internet connection required!

Collect money, with your mobile device, while you are on-the-go. No matter if you have an internet connection or not. All funds will be received and can be spend as soon as you are back online.
Do you need a new bitcoin address to keep track from where you have received funds? No problem... Generate a new one, also if you are offline. Our service assures, that you don't lose any money, no matter if you are online or offline.
